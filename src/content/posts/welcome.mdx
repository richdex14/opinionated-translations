This is your first blog post! The Toggle component lets you hide and reveal text inline.

## Simple Toggle

Click the text below to reveal a hidden message:

<toggle-el>
  <button class="toggle-trigger" style="background:none;border:none;cursor:pointer;color:inherit;text-decoration:underline;text-underline-offset:3px;padding:0 2px;margin:0 1px;font-size:inherit;font-family:inherit">[click to reveal]</button>
  <span class="toggle-content" style="display:none">This text was hidden until you clicked!</span>
</toggle-el>

## Grouped Toggles

You can group toggles together so one button controls multiple blocks. Click the button below to reveal all the "spoiler" content scattered throughout this section:

<toggle-el data-group="spoilers">
  <button class="toggle-trigger" style="background:none;border:none;cursor:pointer;color:inherit;text-decoration:underline;text-underline-offset:3px;padding:0 2px;margin:0 1px;font-size:inherit;font-family:inherit">[Show all spoilers]</button>
  <span class="toggle-content" style="display:none">Spoiler 1: The cake was a lie.</span>
</toggle-el>

Here's some regular text between the spoilers. Nothing hidden here... or is there?

<toggle-el data-group="spoilers">
  <span class="toggle-content" style="display:none">Spoiler 2: There's a secret passage behind the bookshelf.</span>
</toggle-el>

Keep reading... still nothing? Well, maybe:

<toggle-el data-group="spoilers">
  <span class="toggle-content" style="display:none">Spoiler 3: The real treasure was the friends we made along the way.</span>
</toggle-el>

Click "[Show all spoilers]" above again to hide them all.

## Features

- **Static-first**: Pages are pre-rendered for speed
- **MDX content**: Write posts in Markdown with custom elements
- **Toggle component**: Inline show/hide for interactive text
- **Grouped toggles**: One button controls multiple hidden blocks
- **Content collections**: Type-safe content management
